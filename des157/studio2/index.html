Where's Benji?

Benji is gone! Let's retrace our footsteps to find him!

Location 1: Morning Walk at the Hilly Trail

At the crack of dawn, we took Benji out for his morning walk on a hilly trail! He enjoyed his time there and met lots of friendly dogs. He ran up the hills so fast that even I couldn't keep up. Even after this morning walk, Benji was still not tired!

Location 2: Second Morning Walk Around the Neighbourhood

After his first morning walk, he still was not tired so we took him for another walk around our neighbourhood. He ran at the speed of lightning! Wow!! He is a puppy so he has a lot of energy!

Location 3: Playdate With Ginnie

When we came back home, Benji took a nap for a couple of hours. When he woke up, he was fully energized and ready to go out again. When Benji heard that he was going to meet up with his friend Ginnie, he ran so fast up the stairs all the way to Ginnie's house. There, they had a play date.

Location 4: Party at Home

After the play date at Ginnie's house, we threw a party at our house and dressed up Benji. He wore something that my mom had knitted for him as well as a party hat!

Location 5: Neighbourhood Park

Lastly, we took Benji to our neighbourhood park and played fetch and other fun games with him. We were there for a couple of hours and got super tired so we decided to go back home. We were so tired that we didn't even bother to check if Benji had gotten in the car with us! Oh no! Benji must be at the park!

Yay! By retracing our footsteps, we found Benji at the park!
